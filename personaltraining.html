All About Personal Training

Pro Results® Personal Training

STAY ACCOUNTABLE

As a Pro Results® certified personal training client, you’ll get special access to Studio Zone® on the LA Fitness mobile app to guide you through workouts at any time. Track your progress and achieve your goals faster than ever!

ESTABLISH YOUR GOALS

From designing a customized workout plan, to providing ongoing motivation and support, Pro Results® certified personal trainers are dedicated to helping you establish and achieve your personal fitness goals.

MEASURE & TRACK

Pro Results® is ideal for all fitness levels and background. Our certified personal trainers can help you add variety to your workout routine, while also helping you learn new exercises and proper form.We try our best!

What is included in a fitness assessment?

  • A certified professional assessment of your current fitness level.
  • A body composition test, if desired.
  • A personalized timeline to achieve your goals.
  • An overview of cardio & weight equipment.
  • A quick one-on-one workout.
